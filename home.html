What is J-STAR washes about?

J star washes is a black owned buisness that provides premium cleaning services to neighborhoods and buisnesses in need at a cheap & affordable price

Mission Statement

J-STAR Washes aims to make a meaningful difference by innovating neglected areas, eradicating dirt and grime, restoring cleanliness to public spaces and enhancing the visual appeal and cleanliness of communities. We strive to instill a sense of pride and inspire positive change among residents. "Clean environment, Clean head space"
